在

Why drop lambda?  ...  there is a widespread misunderstanding  that lambda can do things that a nested function can't -- ..., I think having the two choices  side-by-side just requires programmers to think about making a choice  that's irrelevant for their program; not having the choice streamlines  the thought process.  Also, once map(), filter() and reduce() are gone,  there aren't a whole lot of places where you really need to write very  short local functions; ...

Guido 认为 lambda 这个名字非常困惑，同时在写程序的时候会思考的连续性。而且，真正在写程序的时候，非常短的函数这种情况很少

Guido 的想法挺有意思。他很在意程序中的可读，不要有很怪异的与程序过程中无关的语法出现，仅仅是为了让代码少一些。
